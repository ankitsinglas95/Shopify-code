Ordering customized labels is easy!

Select a custom product category to get started. Once you find the item you want custom printed, follow these simple steps:

Review software and printer compatibility to make sure the custom item is compatible with your print method.
Download the design template and create your artwork. Artwork not ready yet? You can place an order or create a quote and provide your artwork later.
Select the Buy Now button to place an order, or the Create a Quote button to create an estimate.

Note: You’ll need to login to your Velocity Label account or create a new one to Buy Now or Create a Quote. An account enables you to submit your request, upload artwork, and see the status of your request.
